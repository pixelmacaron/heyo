About The Project

What Did I Have To Do?

For this project, I had to choose a passion, and then study it. I would learn more about it, and present what I figure out to the class. I chose to do coding as my topic. The specific languages that I was going to use were HTML and CSS.

My Goal

I chose this passion because I do not know much about code yet. My goal was to learn about it. I wanted to figure out how websites worked and try to make one by myself. It not only seemed like it would be fun, but I wanted to know what you could do with code. A website sounded nice because you could do a lot with one. For example, there are websites for fun, news, information, learning, and more. There are even more things that I could do with code, such as making a game. Everything on computers is written in code, so if I learned more about it, I could get a better understanding of how computers work.

What I did

For my Passion Project, I created many webpages, some showing the progress of my work, and some explaining and showing how to do what I did. I put a few videos, and some clips of audio. I even added some links, paragraphs, images, backgrounds, and many more parts! I learned a lot of information while working on this project. It was a bit new to me before, but now, I am a bit more familiar with it. I really enjoyed working on this project, and I have learned a lot. I have learned a lot about what you can do with both of the languages.
